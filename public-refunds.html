Refunds Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026

Memberships

Free membership has no charge. Where paid tiers exist, you may cancel anytime; access continues to the end of the billing period.

Merchandise (Shop)

Branded merch is sold via WooCommerce. Unused items in original condition may be returned within 14 days of delivery for a refund or exchange.

  • Faulty or incorrect items: full refund or replacement
  • Change of mind: refund less return shipping
  • Custom items: non-refundable unless faulty

Digital resources

Free downloadable resources are provided as-is and are not refundable, as no payment is taken.

How to request

Email hello@smesouthafrica.co.za with your order number. Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.
