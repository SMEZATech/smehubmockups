Hi Tshepho,

Thank you for reaching out and providing those troubleshooting details. I understand how urgent this is with your pop-up market approaching.

We recently pushed a backend update to the integration API which occasionally requires a hard re-sync from the terminal itself. Please try the following sequence to force a fresh connection token:

  1. Turn the Yoco Neo on.
  2. Hold down the yellow 'Clear' button and the 'Power' button simultaneously for 10 seconds.
  3. The screen will flash and display "Maintenance Mode".
  4. Press 1 (Network Reset).
  5. Once it reboots, open your SME app and initiate a test transaction of R1.00.

If the transaction goes through, you are fully synced. If it still shows offline, please reply here and I will escalate this directly to the hardware engineering team to provision a replacement unit for you before Friday.

Looking forward to your update.
