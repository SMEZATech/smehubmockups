Quick Start Guide

1. Auto-Fill: Paste a link from the SME site and hit "Auto-Fill" to instantly grab the headline and featured image.

2. Format: Choose your campaign type. Standard graphics, IG Carousels, Polls, or WhatsApp layouts.

3. Tweak: Manually edit the text, adjust the typography scale, or toggle the branding off for a cleaner look.

4. Framing: Use the sliders to properly frame the background image if faces are getting cut off.

5. Export: Click Download. For carousels, click through the slides and export them one-by-one to ensure perfect layout rendering.
