Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026

Introduction

SME South Africa ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect, why, and how we protect it, in line with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

Information we collect

Account details (name, email), profile information you choose to add, funding application data you submit, merch order details, and usage data.

How we use your information

To operate the community, match you with funders, process merch orders, send communications you have opted into, and improve the platform.

Sharing

We share data only with service providers needed to run the platform and, with your consent, with funders you choose to apply to. We never sell your data.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to run the site and, with consent, analytics cookies to understand usage. You can decline non-essential cookies at any time.

Your rights

You may access, correct, export or delete your data at any time from Account → Data & POPIA, or by contacting our Information Officer.

Contact

Information Officer: privacy@smesouthafrica.co.za
