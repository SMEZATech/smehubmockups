About Us

Empowering South Africa's founders

SME South Africa is the country's largest community for small and medium business owners — connecting founders with funding, mentorship, events, and the tools to scale.

Our story

Since 2014, we've grown from a resource site into a gated membership community — a place where South African founders ask for help, celebrate wins, and share what works. Today more than 100,000 members rely on the platform.

Our mission

To remove the barriers that hold SMEs back — access to capital, knowledge, and a network that has your back — so more South African businesses can build, grow, and thrive.
