Your Premier Membership Hub for Business Growth

We are the definitive membership platform for South African business owners to access the community, capital, and tools they need to scale. We provide expert guidance, premium digital resources, and direct access to small business financing.

SME South Africa is a unified digital ecosystem designed exclusively to empower local entrepreneurs. We understand the unique landscape of South African business. Our platform equips you with the exact resources, networking opportunities, and funding pathways necessary to navigate every growth stage.

Our journey began in 2014 when digital media entrepreneur Velly Bosega acquired the brand, ushering in a bold, digital-first era. Over the past decade, we've evolved from a standalone publication into a vibrant, interactive membership community, attracting over 100,000 visitors every month.
