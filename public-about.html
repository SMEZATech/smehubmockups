Our Story

Your Premier Membership Hub for Business Growth

Our journey began in 2014 when digital media entrepreneur Velly Bosega acquired the brand, ushering in a bold, digital-first era. Over the past decade, we've evolved from a standalone publication into a vibrant, interactive membership community, attracting over 100,000 visitors every month.

Today, SME South Africa is a single, unified digital ecosystem for South African entrepreneurs — bringing together insight, funding, business solutions, and downloadable resources under one roof, so every founder has a clear path from idea to scale.
